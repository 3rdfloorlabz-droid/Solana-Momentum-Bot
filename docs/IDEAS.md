# Ideas

This file is a parking lot for future TracktaOS and bot expansion ideas. Ideas here are not commitments and should not bypass safety review.

## Wallet Intelligence

- richer wallet balance history
- exposure tracking
- realized and unrealized risk summaries
- wallet/RPC health alerts
- pending reconciliation review flows

## Copy-Trade Engine

- discover high-quality wallets
- classify wallet behavior
- track entry and exit timing
- compare copied trades against paper strategy signals
- add strict liquidity, slippage, and risk caps before any execution work

## Cross-Chain Scanner

- normalize opportunity data across chains
- compare liquidity, volume, holders, and pair quality
- chain-specific safety rules
- chain-specific execution adapters only after observation is stable

## FOMO Integration

- user-facing opportunity dashboard
- alert streams
- configurable strategy profiles
- review workflows for high-volatility candidates
- education and safety overlays

## Ori Modules

- strategy review assistant
- execution-audit explainer
- anomaly triage
- daily research summary
- migration and operations copilot

## Risk Engine

- position sizing recommendations
- daily loss and drawdown simulation
- slippage and route-risk scoring
- duplicate exposure detection
- cooldown and reentry policy analysis
